Basketball

Our basketball team has been working hard this season. The Bruins currently stand at 11 wins and 7 losses in the PAC-12 conference and 21-11 overall. We’ve managed to beat USC in both of our games against them, showing them who’s better. Sadly though, we lost the in the finals of the PAC-12 tournament, but our Bruins will fight on against St. Bonaventure in the the first four of the NCAA tournament tonight!

Football

This year our Bruins finished 4th in the PAC-12 tournament, losing to Kansas State in an upsetting fifteen to thirty-five loss. Overall, UCLA ended up placing 78th nationally. In the upcoming season, the team has already received 28 commits. This includes the country’s thirty-sixth highest rated high school football player, Dorian Thompson-Robinson. The team, excluding freshman and incoming freshman, begin practice this spring. Good luck out there Bruins!
